FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We have answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

Is the certification from Vireon Safety Institute government-recognised?
Yes, Vireon Safety Institute is registered with the Government of India and is ISO Certified. Our certificates are recognized by major industries and government bodies across India and are accepted for employment in reputed companies.
What is the admission process?
The process is simple: (1) Fill in the inquiry form or call us, (2) Attend a free demo class, (3) Complete the admission form, (4) Submit required documents and fees, (5) Start your batch. We try to make it as smooth and transparent as possible.
What is the minimum qualification required to enroll?
For most courses, the minimum requirement is 10+2 (Intermediate). For the Advanced HSE Management program, a graduation degree is preferred. There is no age bar, and candidates from science, arts, or commerce backgrounds can apply.
How does the 100% placement guarantee work?
Our placement team begins working on your profile from day one. We conduct mock interviews, resume workshops, and directly refer students to our 100+ partner companies. If a student does not get placed after completing the course successfully, we continue to support them through additional interviews until they are placed — at no extra cost.
Are there EMI or installment payment options available?
Yes, we offer flexible fee payment plans including EMI installments to make quality safety education accessible to all students. Please contact us directly to discuss a payment plan that suits your financial situation.
What is the expected salary after completing safety courses?
Entry-level safety officers typically earn between ₹20,000 – ₹35,000 per month. With experience and advanced certifications, salaries can range from ₹50,000 – ₹1,20,000 per month. Many of our advanced graduates work in multinational companies with attractive salary packages.
